How to use Taskflow
1
Add a task — Type your task in the text box above the list, then click Add task or press Enter on your keyboard.
2
Mark as done — Click the checkbox on the left of any task, or click the task text itself, to toggle it complete. Click again to mark it undone.
3
Delete a task — Hover over a task to reveal the trash icon on the right, then click it to remove that task permanently.
4
Clear all done tasks — Use the Clear completed button at the bottom to delete every finished task at once.
5
Filter your list — Use the All / Active / Completed buttons to focus on specific tasks.
